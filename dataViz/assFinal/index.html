A story of Mandir and Mandal in Uttar Pradesh

A bi-party contest that converted to a multi-party one, is shifting towards bi-partisanship again.



On Thursday, March the 11th, Uttar Pradesh's results finally came out after a long month of voting. The takeaways from the result in Uttar Pradesh is this: Of the two ambitious political projects that were launched almost simultaneously in the politically turbulent 1990s, Mandal and Mandir, while the second is on its way towards achieving its aim of forging a national community that transcends faultlines of caste, class and region, the first has fragmented, its egalitarian charge more and more dissipated.
